# 1. About the Application

Sporty: Sports Card Scanner is a mobile application that allows users to scan and analyze collectible sports cards such as:

* Baseball cards
* Basketball cards
* Hockey cards
* Other sports trading cards

The App uses image recognition technology to identify card information from photographs taken with a device camera or selected from the device photo library.

The App is designed with a **minimal data collection approach**, meaning it only processes information necessary to provide its functionality.

# 2. Information We Collect

The App does **not collect personal information** such as:

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Postal address
* Account credentials
* Payment details

The App also **does not require users to create accounts**.

However, limited data may be processed to allow the App to function.

## 2.1 Images for Card Scanning

When using the scanning feature, the App may process images captured using your device camera or images selected from your device's photo library.

These images are used solely for:

* Detecting sports cards
* Identifying card information
* Generating scan results

Images are processed only to provide the scanning functionality and are **not used for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes**.

## 2.2 Device Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

**Camera Access**
Used to capture images of sports cards for scanning.

**Photo Library Access**
Used to allow users to select images from their device gallery for analysis.

The App only accesses these resources when the user explicitly chooses to use these features.

# 3. How We Use Information

Information processed by the App is used solely for the following purposes:

* Providing the sports card scanning functionality
* Identifying card details
* Displaying scan results to the user

We do not use user data for:

* Advertising
* Behavioral tracking
* Marketing
* Profiling users

# 4. Third-Party Services

To provide image analysis functionality, the App uses the **Gemini API** provided by **Google**.

When a user scans a sports card, the captured image may be transmitted securely to Google's servers for analysis.

The developer of the App does not control how Google processes data once it reaches Google's infrastructure. Users are encouraged to review Google's privacy policies:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

These third-party services are used strictly to enable the core functionality of the App.

The developer does **not sell or share user data with advertisers or unrelated third parties**.

# 5. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), processing of information is conducted under the legal bases defined by the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**.

The legal basis for processing images within the App is:

**Legitimate Interest**

Processing images is necessary to provide the functionality of scanning and identifying sports cards.

Because the App does not collect personal user accounts or personal identifiers, the processing is limited to what is necessary to provide the requested service.

# 6. Data Retention

The App does not maintain user accounts or a user database.

Images used for card scanning are processed temporarily and are not permanently stored by the App developer.

Users remain in control of their photos and may delete them directly from their device.

# 7. Data Sharing

We do **not sell, rent, trade, or share personal data with advertisers or data brokers**.

Information may only be processed by trusted service providers when necessary to provide the App's functionality, such as the Google Gemini API used for image analysis.

# 8. International Data Transfers

Because the App uses services operated by Google, images submitted for analysis may be transmitted to servers located outside the user's country of residence.

These transfers may include servers located in the United States or other jurisdictions where Google operates infrastructure.

Google implements safeguards designed to protect user data during international transfers.

# 9. Children's Privacy

The App may be used by children.

However, the App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Because the App does not require accounts and does not collect personal identifiers, it is designed to minimize the collection of information from minors.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal information may contact the developer for assistance.
